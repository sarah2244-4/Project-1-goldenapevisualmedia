Hello!

I am Kristian, a passionate photographer with a unique background as a former boxer, and I specialize in freezing time and preserving the magic of both weddings and various events.

Having spent years in the ring, I understand the raw intensity and adrenaline that comes with every punch, every move, and every moment of victory. Now, as a photographer, my goal is to capture that same essence and emotion in every image I create.

From the electric atmosphere of boxing arenas to the thrill of sporting events, I am determined to use my unique perspective as a boxer to bring a fresh and captivating angle to my photography. Just as I once studied my opponents in the ring, I now study the events and athletes before me, seeking out the perfect moments that convey the heart and soul of every occasion.

My transition from boxer to photographer has been a natural evolution, fueled by a passion for storytelling and a desire to share the beauty of sports with the world. Through my lens, I aim to reveal the dedication, discipline, and determination that drives athletes to greatness, inspiring others to push their boundaries and achieve their dreams.

From the tender love stories of weddings to the electric energy of special events, I bring a fresh and captivating perspective to every occasion. My journey from the ring to behind the camera has instilled in me a profound appreciation for emotions, connections, and the beauty of life's most precious moments.

Behind the lens, I am more than just a photographer; I am a dedicated storyteller with a passion for weaving tales through images. I pride myself on my ability to capture the essence of love, the vibrancy of events, and the joy of celebrations, allowing you to relive those cherished memories time and time again.

Whether you're planning your dream wedding or organizing an unforgettable event, I would be honored to be part of your special day. Let's collaborate to create a gallery of images that will forever remind you of the beauty and magic you shared with your loved ones during these milestone moments.

Thank you for considering me as your wedding and events photographer.
